Legal / Privacy

The Privacy Doctrine.

§ 01 — LOCATION TELEMETRY

Live Coordinates.

Broad is built on the live stream of location data. When you are in a "Ride" session, we transmit your GPS coordinates, heading, and speed to our servers. This data is visible only to the riders in your active convoy. We do not store a permanent historical log of your precise movements after a trip is archived, unless you explicitly choose to save it to your personal journal.
§ 02 — LOCAL-FIRST STORAGE

The Glovebox.

Documents uploaded to the Glovebox (licences, registration, etc.) are encrypted on your device using AES-256 before being backed up to our cloud. We do not have the keys to decrypt these documents. Your papers are yours alone; we merely provide the vault.
§ 03 — ZERO ADVERTISING

No Noise.

Broad is funded by its riders, not by data brokers. We do not sell your telemetry, your habits, or your identities to third parties. There are no trackers, no targeted ads, and no "sponsored routes." The road should be free of interruptions.
§ 04 — THIRD-PARTY DISPATCH

Emergency Handoff.

In the event of an SOS trigger, we may transmit your last known location and medical metadata (if provided) to professional emergency services or your designated emergency contacts. This is the only instance where your data is shared outside your chosen convoy.
§ 05 — DATA RETENTION

The Clean Slate.

You can wipe your data at any time. Deleting a trip removes the associated telemetry from our active relays. Deleting your account initiates a full purge of your encrypted Glovebox and profile within 30 days.
