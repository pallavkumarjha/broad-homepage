§ Routes — drawn by riders

The good roads.

Routes we have actually ridden. Distances measured in hours, not kilometres. Each one written so the next rider doesn't have to learn it the hard way.

— 01 / Leh-Ladakh

The road that owes you nothing.

Eight passes above 4,500 metres. A river you'll cross twenty-three times. Towns where the petrol pump is also the only place to sleep.
