How do I invite my crew?
Open a new Ride session, tap the 'Invite' icon, and share the unique 6-digit Convoy Code with your group. They enter it in their app to join the live map.
Riding Together
What happens if I lose signal?
Broad is built for the gaps. We cache your route and documents offline. If you trigger an SOS without signal, the message is queued and sent automatically the moment you find a single bar.
Offline Protocol
How does the SOS detect a crash?
Broad monitors high-frequency telemetry from your phone's gyroscope and GPS. If we detect a sudden deceleration followed by a lack of movement, we'll prompt you. If you don't respond, we alert your convoy.
Safety Systems
Is my document storage secure?
Yes. Your Glovebox documents are encrypted on your phone with AES-256. Even our engineers can't read them. Only your device holds the key to the vault.
Data Security
How do I archive a past ride?
Once a trip ends, go to the 'Journal' tab. You can save the route, photos, and group stats to your permanent archive, or wipe the telemetry clean.
Journals
